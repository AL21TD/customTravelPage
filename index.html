How to Access Your Calls: It's as Easy as 1-2-3!

Getting started with MyTeleDoc.app is a breeze. Here's how you can access your calls in just a few simple steps:

  • 1. Purchase Your Partner Code:
    First, acquire your unique Partner Code. This code is your gateway to seamless medical consultations.
  • 2. Redeem Your Partner Code in the App:
    Once you have your Partner Code, open the MyTeleDoc.app and enter your code in the designated section. This step validates your access and sets you up for consultations.
  • 3. Book Your Appointment:
    With your Partner Code validated, you're all set! Browse through available time slots, select a convenient appointment time, and book your consultation with a qualified healthcare provider.
